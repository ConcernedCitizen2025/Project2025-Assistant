# Nathan Leamer

**Role:** Contributor

**Position:** Vice President of Public Affairs at Targeted Victory

**Background:**  
Nathan Leamer is the Vice President of Public Affairs at Targeted Victory, a political consulting and communications firm. He has extensive experience in technology policy and public affairs. Before joining Targeted Victory, Leamer served as a Policy Advisor to FCC Chairman Ajit Pai during the Trump administration, where he was involved in major regulatory decisions, including the repeal of net neutrality. Prior to his role at the FCC, Leamer was a Senior Fellow at the R Street Institute, where he focused on emerging technology, intellectual property, and privacy issues. He also has experience working as a legislative aide on Capitol Hill, which provided him with a deep understanding of the legislative process and coalition-building.
